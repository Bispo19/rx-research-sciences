About RX Research Sciences

Leading the way in high-purity research compounds since 2010. Our commitment to scientific excellence drives everything we do.

Pioneers in Research Compounds

Founded in 2010, RX Research Sciences began with a simple mission: to provide researchers with the highest quality compounds available. What started as a small laboratory has grown into a global network of scientific excellence.

Our team of pharmaceutical scientists, biochemists, and research specialists work tirelessly to ensure every compound meets our rigorous standards of purity and consistency.

Today, we serve research institutions, universities, and independent researchers in over 50 countries through our network of local representatives.

Our Mission & Values

The principles that guide everything we do at RX Research Sciences

Uncompromising Quality

Every compound undergoes rigorous HPLC testing to ensure 99.9%+ purity. We never compromise on quality, even when it means higher production costs.

Scientific Integrity

We provide accurate, detailed documentation and certificates of analysis for every batch. Transparency in research is non-negotiable.

Global Accessibility

Through our network of local representatives, we make high-quality research compounds accessible to scientists worldwide.

Personalized Support

Each researcher receives dedicated support from our scientific team and local representatives throughout their research journey.

Secure & Discreet

Ethical Standards

We adhere to the highest ethical standards in research compounds, ensuring compliance with all international regulations.
